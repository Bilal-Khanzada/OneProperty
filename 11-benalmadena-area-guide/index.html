Benalmadena

Why choose to Benalmadena?

Benalmadena is a beautiful coastal town on the Costa del Sol, offering the perfect blend of traditional Spanish charm and modern amenities. With stunning beaches, excellent restaurants, vibrant nightlife, and world-class marinas, it's an ideal location for both living and investing.

What to do in Benalmadena

From relaxing on pristine beaches to exploring the historic old town, Benalmadena offers endless activities. Visit the Tivoli World theme park, enjoy water sports, play golf at championship courses, or simply soak up the Mediterranean lifestyle at one of the many beachfront cafes.
